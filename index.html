Crash is your job hunting superpower.

No more applying to hundreds of jobs hoping to hear back from just one. It’s time to put yourself in the driver’s seat.

Job hunting as you know it? Broken in so many ways.

  • Many jobs are filled before they’re even listed on job boards.

    If you’re relying on job boards to find your next thing, you’re missing out on tons of opportunities. You have to be more proactive.

  • The ones that are listed don’t show you what you want to know.

    You want to know what the company culture is like, and what your day-to-day responsibilities would be for the role.

  • You apply with your resume, which doesn’t show your true worth.

    They’re cramped, monotonous, and packed with irrelevant details: “What font size should I use? Should my email come before or after my phone number? What if I have a gap in my work history?” None of this really matters.

  • Applicant Tracking Systems filter for the wrong things.

    Your entire worth as a candidate (and chances of getting an interview) are judged by the system’s ability to understand your resume.

  • “One-Click Apply” is destroying your chances.

    This lazy, “spray-and-pray” approach (applying to hundreds of jobs with the same canned cover letter) tells employers you aren’t any different than the rest of the pile.

  • The entire process feels inhuman and out of your control.

    Sound familiar? “We’re not able to respond to every candidate. If you don’t hear back within 4-6 weeks please try applying for another position in the future.” Yeah, this doesn’t fly with us.

Job hunting with Crash: “The most empowering way” there is.

  • Target specific companies one by one.

    Targeting pitches to specific companies is proven to be more effective in getting their attention.

  • Network with their team members.

    Break into the world of tech and discover who’s in charge. These are the people you want to form relationships with, and Crash helps you get in touch.

  • Prove your real worth to employers.

    Crash pitches put your personality and body of work front-and-center, leaving no doubt about your ability to create value for companies.

  • Keep tabs on all your active opportunities.

    If you want to win, you have to keep following up until you hear back or move on. Your Crash dashboard makes it impossible to forget your opportunities.
